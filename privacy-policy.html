Our Cookies Policy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or actions, to improve your experience.

2. Cookies we use

Paphos-Jivers.com uses Google Analytics cookies. These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, including:

  • Pages visited
  • How long visitors stay on the site
  • Navigation patterns

These cookies are only for analytics purposes and help us understand and improve the website.

3. Data usage

The information collected by Google Analytics is used solely to analyze website traffic and improve the user experience. We do not share this data with advertisers or third parties for marketing purposes.

4. IP Anonymization

All IP addresses are anonymized before being stored by Google Analytics to enhance privacy.

5. Your choices

Before using analytics cookies, we ask for your consent via our cookie banner. You can:

  • Accept cookies to allow Google Analytics to track your visit
  • Decline cookies and prevent Google Analytics from running

You can also change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies.

6. More information

For more information about Google Analytics and how it handles data, please see Google’s Privacy & Terms.
